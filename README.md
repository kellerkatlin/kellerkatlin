### About me

I'm a fullstack engineer shipping software for the **LATAM market** — the part of the world where global SaaS boilerplates fall short.
I build **ecommerce stores**, **SaaS platforms** and **digital products**, plus the **automations** that keep them running.
I lean heavily on **AI-assisted development** to ship faster without losing quality.

### What I do

- 🛒 **Ecommerce** — storefronts, checkout flows, payment + shipping integrations.
- 🧩 **SaaS platforms** — multi-tenant apps, billing, dashboards, role-based access.
- 📦 **Digital products** — checkout, fulfillment and post-sale automation across multiple currencies and gateways.
- ⚙️ **Automations** — workflows, schedulers, webhooks, queues — wiring tools so humans don't have to.
- 🤖 **AI-accelerated development** — I work daily with **Claude Code**, **Claude API** and other LLM tooling: agents, skills, RAG, prompt engineering, and code-gen workflows that compress weeks of work into days.
